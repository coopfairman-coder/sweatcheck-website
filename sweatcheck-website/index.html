Founder Story

WHY I
BUILT THIS.

I'm Cooper Fairman — founder of SweatCheck, senior finance major at the University of Tennessee, and someone who has been in the gym consistently since I was 15.

Here's what I kept noticing: the gym is the one place most people want to be consistent, and fail at consistently. Not because they don't care. Because there's no social pressure. Nobody sees when you skip. Nobody sees when you show up for 47 straight days.

"If your fitness journey was public, how much harder would you push?"

That question became SweatCheck. Public Sweat Streaks your whole network sees. A gym-aware focus mode called Lock In that kills distractions the moment you walk through the doors. And the Vault — a marketplace built into every profile so creators can earn from the community they're building.

This isn't another fitness tracker. It's the first social platform designed for the gym from day one. We're launching soon. Be one of the first 1,000.

Cooper Fairman
Founder & CEO, SweatCheck Co.
