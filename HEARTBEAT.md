## Schedule

## Morning Format

🌅 [Date]
**Focus today:** [The one thing that matters most]
**Also:** [1-2 secondary items, one line each]
**Blocked:** [Anything stuck, or "Clear"]
**Reminder:** [Only if there's a calendar event, deadline, or time-sensitive item. Otherwise omit entirely.]

## Evening Format

🌙 [Date]
**Moved forward:** [What progressed]
**Slipped:** [What didn't, or "Nothing"]
**Tomorrow:** [Top focus]

## Rules

- No greetings. No signoffs. No "Hope you had a productive day."
- If nothing changed since morning, evening = "No movement. Same priorities." — that's it.
- Heartbeats exist to keep Cooper oriented, not to perform productivity.
- If a heartbeat would be empty fluff, skip it entirely. Silence > noise.
