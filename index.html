The Origin Story

BUILT BY GYM RATS.
FOR GYM RATS.

Two college roommates walked out of the gym one afternoon and asked a simple question: why is there no home for gym culture online? Fitness content was buried in TikTok rabbit holes and Instagram aesthetics — scattered, diluted, disposable. There was no platform built purely for people who actually train. So they built it.

CF
Cooper Fairman
CEO & Co-Founder

A high school athlete who didn't make the next level — and never stopped training because of it. Cooper is obsessed with fitness, obsessed with building, and hell-bent on creating the platform he always wished existed. SweatCheck isn't a startup to him. It's a calling.
